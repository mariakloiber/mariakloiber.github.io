Gender and Political Party as Predictors for Attitudes towards Homosexuality

A statistical study conducted in R, Nov - Dec 2016

This was a research and statistical study conducted in R. I used t-tests, chi-square tests, linear regression tests and ANOVA tests of variance to analyze data from an annual survey conducted in Houston amongst the city’s residents. My aim was to explore the relationship between attitudes towards homosexuality and gender, and attitudes towards homosexuality and political party.

Polethia.com

A bipartisan domestic and international political news site, Aug 2015 - Nov 2016

After attending a Model UN training program at Georgetown University, I collaborated with several other students from the program to develop a site that would provide concise but comprehensive profiles on the 2016 presidential election candidates. As we were a group of students from across the country with a variety of different political beliefs, we were able to make sure that the information provided on each candidate was fair and unbiased. Since then, we have expanded to detailing different international events and analyzing both sides of popular domestic policy issues.
